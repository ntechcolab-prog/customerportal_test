Frequently Asked Questions

We compiled a list of answers to address your most pressing questions regarding our Services.

How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team by email at support@netzsch.com or by using the Contact form available in your portal account. Our team is available Monday–Friday, 8am–6pm CET.
What services do you offer?
The NETZSCH Customer Portal provides asset management, order management for grinding beads, real-time delivery tracking, service case submission and tracking, and team role management — all in one platform.
What types of products are offered?
Through our Online Shop you can order NETZSCH Grinding Beads and related grinding conditions. We offer a full range of bead sizes and materials optimized for your specific grinding and dispersing applications.
What types of contents are offered?
The portal provides technical documentation, order history, delivery status reports, service case updates, and equipment performance insights — giving you full visibility over your NETZSCH assets and operations.
